Wireless Key Fob

The Alertex Wireless Key Fob can activate the Alertex remotely up to 100m away from a repeating unit.

  • Activates the Alertex system
  • Up to 2 year battery life
  • Up to 100m wireless transmission range
  • Supplied with breakaway lanyard
